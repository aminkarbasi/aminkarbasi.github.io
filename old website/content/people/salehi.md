Mehraveh is currently Chief Experience Officer at Summary Analytics. She is interested in combinatorial optimization and machine learning algorithms with a focus on submodularity and its applications in the human brain. Her Ph.D. thesis focused on developing predictive models that relate human behavior to individual brain functional connectivity patterns as measured by fMRI. She enjoys playing Santour (a Persian music instrument), watching movies, and running.
