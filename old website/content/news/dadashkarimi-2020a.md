Javid Dadashkarimi won [Brain Initiative](https://www.braininitiative.org/events/pimeeting/) Trainee Award for `A web-based toolkit for visualizing and interpreting complex connectomic results in [BISWeb](https://bioimagesuiteweb.github.io/webapp/connviewer.html) '. This award is given to students who had significant research impact that can create a dynamic understanding of brain function.
